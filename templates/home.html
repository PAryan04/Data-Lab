Turning Data into Decisions

Data Analytics is the systematic process of collecting, cleaning, and analyzing data to extract meaningful insights and support informed decision-making. It involves working with different types of data such as cross-sectional data and time-series data, and applying descriptive statistics to summarize and understand data characteristics. Using inferential statistics, data analytics draws conclusions and makes generalizations about larger populations. Through effective data visualization, complex patterns and trends are communicated clearly, while techniques such as linear regression are used for prediction and forecasting to support data-driven strategies.
